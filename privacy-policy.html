Privacy Policy for "Advanced Copy-Paste Tabs"

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or browsing history.

How It Works

All data (including URLs and tab information) stays locally within your browser.
No information is sent to any external servers or third-party services.

Permissions

The permissions required are solely to:

  • Access the tabs (to list open tab URLs)
  • Copy to clipboard (to export URLs)
  • Read clipboard content (to open URLs on paste)

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share or sell any user data because we do not collect any.
